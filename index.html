04 — The Market

Why Brazil. Why now.

#1
Real estate market in Latin America
01
Economic growth momentum

Brazil's GDP is outpacing much of the developed world. Expanding middle class, growing domestic tourism and a booming commodity sector are driving sustained demand for quality real estate.

02
Undervalued coastal land

Comparable coastal land in Southern Europe or the Caribbean trades at 5–10x the price of equivalent Brazilian locations. Maricá is at an early stage of the development cycle — maximum upside.

03
Infrastructure investment wave

Petrobras infrastructure has transformed Maricá's economy. The time to buy is before the highway arrives.

04
Exceptional FX opportunity for EUR/USD investors

For European and American investors, the current exchange rate means Brazilian real estate represents outstanding purchasing power — hard assets priced in a historically weaker currency.
