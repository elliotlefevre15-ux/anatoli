05 — The Location

Why Maricá.
Why now.

#1
Real estate market in Latin America
01
Fastest-growing municipality in Rio state

Maricá's GDP grew over 12% annually — driven by Petrobras infrastructure investment and one of the most unspoilt coastlines in Southeast Brazil.

02
Undervalued vs comparable coastal markets

Comparable coastal property in Southern Europe or the Caribbean trades at 5–10x the price of equivalent Maricá locations. You are buying at the beginning of the curve.

03
Infrastructure wave already underway

Federal and state governments are investing heavily in the Rio–Maricá corridor. Roads already built on site. The time to buy is before the highway arrives.

04
Exceptional FX opportunity for EUR/USD buyers

For European and American buyers, the current exchange rate means outstanding purchasing power — hard assets priced in a historically weaker currency.
