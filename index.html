05 — The Investment

Why Brazil.
Why now.

#1
Real estate market
in Latin America
01
Brazil's fastest-growing coastal market

Maricá's GDP has grown over 12% annually, driven by major Petrobras infrastructure investment and the expansion of the Rio–Maricá corridor. The fundamentals are in place — the opportunity is now.

02
Undervalued against global comparables

Coastal property in Southern Europe or Southeast Asia trades at 5–10x the price of equivalent Brazilian locations. For international buyers, the value proposition is exceptional — and the window is narrowing.

03
Infrastructure already under construction

Federal and state investment in the Rio–Maricá corridor is already underway. Roads are built on site. The time to enter a market is before the infrastructure arrives — not after.

04
Exceptional FX advantage for EUR & USD buyers

For European and American investors, the current exchange rate represents outstanding purchasing power. Hard assets in a growing economy — priced in a historically weaker currency.
